=============================
Metadata at the dataset level
=============================

CKAN allows administrators to customize the metadata on the basis of the characteristics of
collected datasets. In the |site_name|, we have organized the metadata at the dataset level
into three categories: ``Basic Information``, ``Descriptive Information``, and
``Management Information``.
